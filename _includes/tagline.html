Made in America

When the government spends your tax dollars on buying goods, they need to be American-made.

“Made in America” is designed to increase reliance on domestic supply chains and reduce the need to buy from foreign suppliers using waivers from Made in America Laws. Establishing a consistent process across agencies and gathering data on use of such waivers will support decision-making and a more resilient U.S. supply chain. Domestic producers, current or potential, can use this site to find information on what has been bought in the past using waivers and what is currently proposed for purchase under waivers.
